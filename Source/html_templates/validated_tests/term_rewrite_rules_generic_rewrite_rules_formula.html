\( \newcommand{\Vnorm}{\downdownarrows} \)

Assuming that \((\xi,t,D) = \mathsf{Skel}(\ell,p)\) for some \(\ell \rightarrow r \in \mathcal{R}\) and \(p\) a position of \(\ell\), and assuming that \(\mathit{root}(\ell) = f\), generic_rewrite_rules_formula \((\zeta \vdash^? u)\) \((\xi,t,D)\) directly applies the deduction fact \(\zeta \vdash^? u\) on the formulas generated by the skeleton, i.e., \(\mathsf{RewF}(\xi,\ell \rightarrow r,p)\Vnorm\) where \(t\) and \(D\) are used for the protocol terms.

We also adopt the following convention:

  • Free first-order (resp. second-order variables) will be denoted by \(w\) (resp. \(W\) )
  • Existential first-order (resp. second-order variables) will be denoted by \(x\) or \(y\) (resp. \(X\) or \(Y\) )
  • Universal first-order (resp. second-order variables) will be denoted by \(z\) (resp. \(Z\) )
  • Public names will be denoted by \(a\) or \(b\) or \(c\)
  • Bound names will be denoted by \(k\) or \(l\) or \(m\)
