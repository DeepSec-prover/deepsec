Tests to validate for the function Term.Rewrite_rules.skeletons

skeletons \(u\) \(f\) \(k\) returns the list of skeletons \((f,X,\xi,t,D)\) such that \( (\xi,t,D) = \mathsf{Skel}(\ell,p)\), \(\mathit{vars}(\xi) \in \mathcal{X}^2_k\), \(\ell \rightarrow r \in \mathcal{R}\), \(\mathit{root}(\ell) = f\), \(X = \xi|_p\), \(\ell|_p \not\in \mathcal{X}^1\), \(\mathit{mgs}(\ell|_p =^? u) \neq \bot\) and \( \mathit{vars}(\ell|_p) \cap \mathit{vars}(r) \neq \emptyset\). Note that we consider that \(\mathsf{Skel}(\ell,p)\) is unique with fresh variable.

We also adopt the following convention:

  • Free first-order (resp. second-order variables) will be denoted by \(w\) (resp. \(W\) )
  • Existential first-order (resp. second-order variables) will be denoted by \(x\) or \(y\) (resp. \(X\) or \(Y\) )
  • Universal first-order (resp. second-order variables) will be denoted by \(z\) (resp. \(Z\) )
  • Public names will be denoted by \(a\) or \(b\) or \(c\)
  • Bound names will be denoted by \(k\) or \(l\) or \(m\)
